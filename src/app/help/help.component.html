Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1.How can I place an order?

    option 1

    Click on your choice of Restaurant ,Browse the dishes, add to cart, and go to checkout to place your order.

    option 2

    You can search your dish , you will get restaurants which are delivering your searched dish.

  • 2.How do I track my order?

    You can track your order status from the “Orders” page after login.

  • 3.Can I cancel or modify my order?

    Yes, within 5 minutes of placing the order via the “Orders” section.
